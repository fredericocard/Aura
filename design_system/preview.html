01 · Brand

The mark

The Aura sigil is an ellipse silhouette containing two mirrored triangular "A" slivers and a small seed near the base. Inline SVG, scales cleanly. Use --forest on parchment, --parchment on ink, --gold on deep-ink Memory Card.

03 · Categories

Trait colors

Five traits, five colors, forever. Each also has a -soft variant (parchment-mixed fill) for tinted backgrounds. Crimson is special — it signals social friction (rivalry, bracket violations) and should feel weighty. Never reassign these.

Brilliance
Clarity, clever plays, the smartest move of the night.
#C99B2F · --cat-brilliance
Flavour
Style, creativity, the deck that felt like theatre.
#7E4E8A · --cat-flavor
Rivalry
The target on your back. The archenemy of the table.
#9E2B2B · --cat-rivalry
Allegiance
Politics, alliances, the best handshake of the game.
#2F7A74 · --cat-allegiance
Fun
Joy, vibe, the one you'd invite back first.
#E07B4A · --cat-fun

07 · Shadows

Warm-tinted depth

Always rgba(43,33,24,*), never pure black. Shadow is how ceremony is signaled — rest for quiet cards, active for the current focus, memory for the reveal.

--shadow-rest · default
--shadow-active · focus card
--shadow-memory · Memory Card

11 · Motion

Durations

Always cubic-bezier(.22,.61,.36,1). No linear, no spring, no bounce.

--dur-fast · 80ms
Tap feedback, toggle flip.
--dur-base · 160ms
Hover, selection, opacity fade.
--dur-slow · 240ms
Screen transitions, drawer open.
--dur-memory · 420ms
Memory Card reveal. One moment only.
